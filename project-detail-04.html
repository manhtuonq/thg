Bookipedia - Nguyen Trai High School for the Gifted Book Club

A 6-year-old book club dedicated to promoting reading culture and creative expression through literary events and sharing book sessions.

About the Project

As Head of Event Department, I led a team of 20 organizers by delegating tasks, ensuring smooth logistics, and fostering a strong sense of teamwork. I organized 2 book-sharing sessions aimed at promoting reading among high school students, and developed detailed event roadmaps to guide the planning process. To ensure successful execution, I coordinated with guest speakers, sponsors, and venue providers, balancing creativity with structure in every stage of the event.

At our club’s annual flower sale event celebrating Vietnamese Women’s Day on October 20th, we were delighted to receive a large number of orders— 96 in total. As the person in charge of coordinating and assigning personnel for each stage of the flower-folding process, I was responsible for ensuring that everything ran smoothly and efficiently. This experience allowed me to strengthen my event coordination skills, even in a lively and unpredictable setting

The Reading Festival attracted over 300 students and generated strong engagement through interactive activities such as book-themed games, quizzes, and lucky draws. The event successfully promoted reading culture within the school and strengthened the club’s connection with the student community.
