NCC - Nguyen Trai High School for the Gifted Chess Club

- Head of Media Department -

My club is the newest club in the school. But in just 2 years, with the aim of creating a healthy playground for students who are passionate about Chess, we have created a buzz by organizing 2-3 tournaments a year, from school level to provincial level.

About the Project

Through strategic social media campaigns and direct outreach , I successfully promoted both school-level and city-level chess tournaments, increasing our page followers by over 21% and maintaining an average engagement rate of 60%. Each tournament attracted more than 100 participants. Beyond promotion, I provided guidance to a team of 15 members, helping them identify and develop their individual strengths. I also led the club’s creative direction — designing visual assets and crafting media content to enhance our image and community engagement
